Key Gender Issues in the Philippines

Understanding the challenges is the first step toward creating meaningful change in our society.

Gender-Based Violence

Despite legal protections, many Filipinos still face domestic violence, sexual harassment, and other forms of gender-based violence.

Workplace Inequality

Gender pay gaps, discrimination in hiring practices, and barriers to leadership positions remain significant challenges.

LGBTQ+ Rights

LGBTQ+ Filipinos continue to face discrimination, with limited legal protections and social acceptance in many communities.
