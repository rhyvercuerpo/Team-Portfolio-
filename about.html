Empowering Gender Equality in the Philippines

Welcome to GED (Gender Equality and Development), an informative website committed to promoting a fair, just, and inclusive society where everyone — regardless of gender — is respected, valued, and empowered.

Our mission is to raise awareness and advocate for real change by educating the public on key issues such as gender-based violence, workplace inequality, and the many challenges still faced by women, LGBTQIA+ individuals, and other marginalized groups in the Philippines.

We believe that knowledge is power. Through facts, real-life stories, and accessible resources, we strive to inform and inspire people of all ages to become agents of change in their own communities.

By understanding the root causes of gender inequality — from harmful stereotypes to systemic discrimination — we can take bold steps toward building a society that celebrates diversity and prioritizes equal opportunity.

Join us in our journey toward empowering gender equality in the Philippines.
